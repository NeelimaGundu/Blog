Some brands to get inspired by!

This article shares some interesting brands that are eco-friendly and support women by employing them. Read their great stories and get inspired by the women whose lives got better.
Tap to read.

1. PHOOL.Co

Kanpur based Phool.co is a great solution for the tonnes of floral waste that is thrown out in the various seas and rivers all arounf the India. Phool is doing its part to clean up the River Ganges by recycling flowers from temples and mosques. These sacred flowers are handcrafted into charcoal-free incense, organic vermicompost and biodegradable packaging material through our ‘Flowercycling’ technology. It is founded by Ankit Agarwalto. Moreover, all of their products are handcrafted by the women flowercyclers, providing them with predictable and healthy livelihoods. So when you buy their products, you are not just buying them, you are giving those women a good livelihood. Is'nt this something inspiring?

2.Paalaguttapalle Bags

This is a brand, rose from a group of women from a village called Paalaguttapalle in Chittoor district,Andhra Pradesh. After drought struck, and their agriculture slowed down, they started stitching to sustain their families. The quality of their bags is admired by all, and they have supplied to many conferences, events and functions. They also make tasty pickles, free of preservatives, using traditional recipies. They make different designed bags, vegetable storing bags to keep in fridge. The bags are made from absolutely eco-friendly 'jute'. Aren't these pretty and good enough to buy because this also supports those women who work hard!
